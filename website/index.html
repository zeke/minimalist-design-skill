Five principles

01  One font, one size. IBM Plex Mono at 1rem everywhere.

02  Opacity creates hierarchy. 100%, 60%, 35%.

03  Stone palette by default. Swap for zinc, slate, or neutral.

04  No shadows, gradients, or decorative borders.

05  System dark mode. No JavaScript.

Typography

Weight and opacity. Nothing else.

font-medium — headings, labels, emphasis

font-normal — body text, default

opacity-60 — secondary, descriptions

opacity-35 — hints, tertiary content

italic — captions, asides, annotations
